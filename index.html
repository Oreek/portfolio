Some questions I get asked a lot IRL

Is your whole body completely white by birth? Is it an illness? Can it be cured?

No, I have a condition called Oculocutaneous albinism (OCA) which causes loss of pigment in my skin throughout my body, making everything from my hair to my eyes appear very white. It is not an illness or disease but rather just a loss of 'melanin' causing me issues in sunlight, and hence has no cure for it. You should avoid asking this question to me IRL because answering the same has become way too annoying now.
